Optimize your monitoring services A monitoring service could be keeping your app awake unintentionally. Run heroku logs to view your app's logs. If needed, remove Pingdom, New Relic, or other availability monitoring services that are not required.

Check your app for worker dynos Your app may not be running a web dyno. Once worker-only apps are started, they run continuously. You can avoid this by upgrading to Hobby dynos or running heroku ps:stop <process-type> on your app.
